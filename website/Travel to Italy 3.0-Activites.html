Cook with us in rome

Join Gianni and Cesare, two local Romans and long time best friends on a culinary journey in Rome! Gianni and Cesare host cooking classes and food tours. For the cooking class, you will meet your hosts in the morning, and your day will start out at wandering around Rome's most famous food market. You will help shop for your ingredients and then you will walk to their studio located a few blocks from the center to cook your Italian feast! There is also an option for an evening class if you want your day free to see the sites. Since the market is closed in the afternoons, the evening tour meets directly at our studio. The rate for the cooking class includes all ingredients for your 4-course lunch or dinner, and even includes wine. A sample menu is a Roman-style artichoke for antipasto, 2 or 3 types of pasta made by hand (cavatelli, fettucine and ravioli) and 2 desserts.
